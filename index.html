Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if I received a Notice to Appear?

Contact an immigration attorney immediately. The sooner we review your case, the more options you may have to stop removal.

Can deportation be stopped?

In many cases, yes. Depending on your situation, we can request stays of removal, reopen cases, or file emergency motions.

Do I need a lawyer for immigration court?

While it's not required, having an attorney significantly increases your chances of a successful outcome in court.

Is my information confidential?

Yes. Everything you share is fully confidential and protected under legal privacy standards.
